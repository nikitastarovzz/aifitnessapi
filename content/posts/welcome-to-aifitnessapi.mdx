Health and fitness software is having a moment. AI coaching, computer-vision
form tracking, wearables, recovery scoring, personalized nutrition — the tools
that used to require a research lab now ship in a weekend. But building a
product people actually stick with is still hard, and most of that difficulty
is invisible from the outside.

**AIFitnessAPI is a hub for the people doing that work.** Founders, engineers,
designers, and operators building — or evaluating — products in health,
wellness, and fitness tech.

## What you'll find here

We're focused on the practical, not the hype:

- **Product breakdowns** — how real fitness and wellness apps are built, from
  onboarding to retention loops.
- **API deep-dives** — honest looks at the platforms and APIs you can build on,
  what they do well, and where they fall short.
- **Playbooks** — engagement, gamification, AI coaching, and the unglamorous
  infrastructure that makes them work.

## Who it's for

If any of these sound like you, you're in the right place:

1. You're **building** a fitness, wellness, or health app and want to skip the
   undifferentiated plumbing.
2. You're **evaluating** APIs and platforms and want a straight answer instead
   of a sales page.
3. You're **curious** about where this space is going and want signal over noise.

> The best fitness products aren't the ones with the most features — they're
> the ones people come back to. Everything we write comes back to that.
